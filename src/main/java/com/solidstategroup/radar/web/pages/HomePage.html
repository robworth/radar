RaDaR

Welcome to the home page of the UK Registry for Rare Kidney Diseases, acronym: RaDaR.

Read more about RaDaR and the rare diseases initiative at www.rarerenal.org

RaDaR is the site where healthcare professionals can register patients and enter data. Patients who are registered with RaDaR can look up their test results and info at PatientView.

Click for a list of diseases that can be registered, criteria and consent forms rarerenal.org/join/criteria-and-consent

RaDaR is an initiative of the Renal Association and the British Association for Paediatric Nephrology. It operates within the UK Renal Registry, and its governance lies with the Renal Association. The initiative is supported by the Kidney Research UK and the British Kidney Patients Association.
